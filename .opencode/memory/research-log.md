# Research Log

Lab-notebook style append-only log of research milestones, decisions in motion, and observations that don't yet rise to the level of `decisions.md`.

**Tier 1 (permanent).** Never rotated. See `ROTATION.md`.

## Format

Each entry: `## YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM — <one-line summary>` followed by a free-form note. No required schema; this is a notebook, not a record.

## Use this file when

- A `/think` session produced a sub-branch worth tracking but not yet locked
- A paper read changed the assessment of a research direction
- A meeting / advisor conversation updated the research agenda
- An experiment / pilot produced a non-publishable but informative result

## Use `decisions.md` instead when

- A `/deep-dive` or `/plan` run formally PROCEEDed past So-What Gate
- A methodological choice is locked for a paper-in-progress
- A direction was REJECTed with finality (also append to `failed-ideas.md`)
