## Search Tips

- **Journal-specific search**: On Semantic Scholar, filter by venue using `venue: "Computers & Education"`
- **Conference papers**: Conference papers typically have lower citation counts than journal articles, but this does not imply lower quality; best paper awards at AIED/LAK are worth special attention
- **Newer journals**: *Computers & Education: Artificial Intelligence* is a relatively new journal with fewer papers but high quality — do not dismiss articles due to lower citation counts
